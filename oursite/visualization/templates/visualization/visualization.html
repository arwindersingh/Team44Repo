Comparison and Analysis


In this project, we try to find some relationship betweet tweets toward vegan and the local health data.
Though we roughly compared the vegan tweets with the official data we collected from aurin, this is only an inspiring discovery for further research. Our discovery can’t explain the co relation of the relationship we discovered. First, the size of our sample is not big enough so it is maybe biased. Second, our data is not normalised. The number of tweets can’t exactly reflect the number of users(vegan/vegetarian) as they may tweets several times. Also, our project only focused on the twitter user, as it can be found in Instruction part that the twitter users has a special demographic characteristic, the sample people are different from that in the whole population.
